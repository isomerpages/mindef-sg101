<p>Reunion dinner, or 團圓飯 (<em>tuán yuán fàn</em>), is a cherished time when
families come together during Lunar New Year. As a symbol of solidarity
and cohesiveness, reunion is often celebrated over a steamboat meal in
Singapore.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/474813488_1054931146674858_4541564335705161759_n.jpg">
</div>
<p>For most reunion dinners, it is common to see a bubbling steamboat at
our dining tables! Steamboat is more than just a meal - from buying our
favourite ingredients, to clinking chopsticks and the joyful anticipation
of loved ones gathered around.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/475053451_1054931186674854_741631408339889304_n.jpg">
</div>
<p>Many of the steamboat ingredients hold special and auspicious meaning!
More than just a meal; the steamboat is a vessel of tradition and reminder
of the importance of family. The shared experience of passing plates and
relishing each other's company levels up a simple dinner into a celebration
of togetherness.</p>
