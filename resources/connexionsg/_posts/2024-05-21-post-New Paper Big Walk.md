<p>Several editions of the Big Walk followed in the years after: some in
collaboration with other partners, or in conjunction with milestone celebrations
such as the 50th anniversary of Singaporeâ€™s independence in 2015.</p>
<p>For many Singaporeans who participated, this event holds a special place
in their hearts. It was a day where people from all walks of life came
together to celebrate fitness, camaraderie, and community spirit. Participants
have fond memories of lacing up their sneakers and hitting the streets
of Singapore, exploring scenic routes and iconic landmarks along the way.
It allowed people to come together to celebrate Singapore's multiculturalism
and inclusivity.</p>
