<p>On 1st of July every year, we commemorate the Singapore Armed Forces (SAF)
Day. It is a day to rededicate ourselves to the defence of Singapore and
reflect on our roles and responsibilities to collectively keep this nation
strong.</p>
<p>Today’s world is fraught with conflicts and threats. The ongoing Russia-Ukraine
and Israel-Hamas conflicts remind us not to take peace for granted. Closer
to home, the police station terror attack in Johor and cases of self-radicalisation
in Singapore are reminders that the threat of terrorism remains high. Increasingly
complex threats in the digital domain can also impinge on our sovereignty
and security.</p>
<p>To ensure that it is well-prepared to meet the challenges ahead, the SAF
continues to transform and enhance its capabilities. A competent SAF, together
with a resilient and united Singapore citizenry, will keep us safe and
strong today and into the future.</p>
<p>To those who are serving or have served – thank you for your commitment
and contributions to the defence of Singapore! Happy SAF Day!</p>
