<p>From PayNow to Singpass, life’s gotten smarter and faster in Singapore.
But with greater digital convenience comes greater cyber risk.</p>
<p>This week, Coordinating Minister for National Security K Shanmugam reminded
us: Cyber threats are evolving; and so must we.&nbsp;</p>
<p>As the digital world gets more complex, protecting ourselves won’t get
easier. That’s why good cyber hygiene isn’t optional — it’s essential.</p>
<p>Let’s stay vigilant, stay safe, and build a secure digital future together.&nbsp;</p>
